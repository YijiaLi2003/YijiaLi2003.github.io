Owl Hunt

Playdate game where the crank controls aiming, diving, and returning with prey.

Playdate Lua Game Feel

Role

Solo developer — gameplay design, tuning, and Lua implementation.

Design Goal (True North)

Deliver a tense, atmospheric hunting experience where the crank embodies the owl’s struggle for survival — aiming, diving, and returning with prey all reinforce effort, weight, and urgency.

What I Built

  • Crank-driven aiming arc and commit-to-dive control loop designed for short, intense sessions
  • Return phase that requires cranking backward to “fight” prey weight and reach safety
  • Multiple prey behaviors (rats vs rabbits) to vary difficulty and decision-making
  • Invisible dynamic difficulty (prey speed, night length, hearts) to keep tension high without menus
  • Minimal UI + hint system that teaches through feedback rather than text tutorials
